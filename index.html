RECHARGE-OK: Revitalizing Oklahoma with Electric Energy

Recharge-OK is an innovative project designed to enhance Oklahoma's energy resilience through the integration of Electric School Buses (ESB) with Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) technology, promoting sustainable energy use.

Technology Explanation

What is V2G Technology?

Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) technology allows electric vehicles to return power to the grid, providing backup power and enhancing grid stability. This makes electric school buses perfect candidates for pilot V2G initiatives due to their predictable schedules and large battery capacity.

In addition, V2G technology works by connecting the vehicle's battery to the grid when it is not in use, allowing for energy to be drawn or provided based on the grid's needs. The ability to use stored energy from multiple vehicles can reduce the reliance on traditional power plants, especially during peak demand times, and stabilize the overall power system. Electric school buses, with their consistent schedules, are an ideal platform for this pilot because they provide a reliable energy storage solution when not transporting students.
